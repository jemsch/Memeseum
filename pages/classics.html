Weegee

Weegee is a famous iconic Mario fancharacter who was created in 2007. His design is based on the odd sprite of Luigi from the DOS game Mario is Missing. On August 11 2007, the user MonkeyInCloset stated about his ugly art style design. He also posted a vectorized sprite on the same day. Months later, a 4chan user Moot declared Weegee to be his favorite 4chan meme. In the couple following years Weegee quickly spread through several websites.

Rickroll

A rickroll is a form of clickbait that is a unexpect redirect to the 1987 music video "Never Gonna Give You Up". On August 31 2006, the youtuber Erik Helwig claimed to have a prank where he played "Never Gonna Give You Up" over a phone. One of the earliest known examples of Rickroll was on May 15 2007 by the youtuber Cotter548.

Youtube Poop

A youtube poop is a type of video edit from pre-exisiting media for humorous, shocking, evil, good, or other purposes. In 2004, SuperYoshi uploaded an edit of The Adventures of Super Mario Bros. However, it wasn't uploaded to Youtube until November 2006. Not so long later, several fans began to edit and upload bizzare videos.
