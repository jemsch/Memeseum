Reaction Images

Conceited Reaction

An American rapper known as Reggie Conceited Sergil from Brooklyn, New York, happens to be the main subject of an image macro meant for expressing a slight disapproval or mockery to a situation. The image originated from a rap battle between Jesse James and Conceited which was later uploaded to Youtube on January 13, 2013, causing it to soon spread to other forms of social media.

Roll Safe

Actor Kayode Ewumi plays Reece Simpson in Hood Documentary, a web series that the meme comes from. The BBC Three channel on Youtube uploaded a mini documentary of the series on June 1st, 2016. The actor is shown pointing to his head with a slight smile within a screenshot of the video and later it became a popular image to caption with words which imply what high intelligence there is over decisions of low common sense.

Unsettled Tom

This meme originates from an animated comedy series founded by William Hanna and Joseph Barber titled Tom and Jerry. The two protagonists are in a constant state of rivalry and one of these characters happens to be the representation of this meme. “Unsettled Tom” is a screenshot taken during the year 2012 from a comic based off of the show Tom and Jerry. The template people use for this meme includes a statement to provide a simple action and another line after to add a shocking element with context to the scene.

Suprised Pikachu

Derived from the Japanese title, Pocket Monsters, Pokemon is an animated Japanese television series, premiered April 1st of 1997. The character Pikachu has been screenshotted with a surprised expression from Episode 10 of season 1, Bulbasaur and the Hidden Village during the time mark 3:05. September 26, 2018 was the day that this meme has been first used. Within the next few days, the meme spread through the internet.

Drake Posting

Aubrey Drake Graham, the Canadian rapper born during 1986, became the subject of this meme trend after his release of Hotline Bling. The January of 2016 began with the trend of using screenshots from his music video Hotline Bling as reaction images. The trend started off being popular among 4chan users and later spread to Instagram, Twitter and more.

Side Eyeing Chloe

A vlog on youtube with the title of Lily's Disneyland Surprise… AGAIN was posted September 12, 2013 by KAftC which features the sisters Chloe and Lili. After finding out that they would go to Disneyland, Lili is excited while Chloe has an expression of disgust shown in a few quick frames. This image has been screenshotted and used for captions or photoshopped edits. Side Eyeing Chloe has been photoshopped over many celebrities faces and even has the image of her at Google in Sao Paulo. Buzzfeed, Seventeen, and Bustle have published articles with images relating to Chloe’s growth over the past few years in comparison to her age shown in the meme.
